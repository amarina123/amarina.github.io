Why Join?

Create Trash Into Planes and More.

Recycling Trash

With over 7,000 bags of trash gathered, with every piece of origami you eliminate the litter on the streets. From old newspapers to sheets of paper, instead of a dog or cow eating it, you can create a multitude of things from folding the paper.

Origami

The trash gathered turns into origami. Most of the children residing in the slums didn't even know what origami was until we demonstrated it with a plane. This initiates the creative mind, and gives a meaningful way to recycle paper that is easily accesible.

Learning

Through origami we want to explain how the things we make work. I used paper planes to instill the lessons of aerodynamics in young minds and create a passion for aerospace engineering. Most of these children have never seen a boat, and only see planes in the sky so through this they can relate to it on another level./p>
